Core Architecture

D-CNN Residual Blocks

Our engine utilizes a Deep Residual Convolutional Neural Network. Unlike standard CNNs, our residual blocks allow the model to learn identity mappings, preventing the "vanishing gradient" problem during deep MRI slice training.

layer_1: Conv3D(32, (3,3,3))
layer_2: BatchNormalization()
layer_3: ReLU()
// Residual Connection
layer_4: Add([l3, l1])
