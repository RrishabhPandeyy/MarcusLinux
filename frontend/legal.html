Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 28, 2026

Data Minimization & PHI

NeuroScanAI adheres to strict HIPAA guidelines. We do not permanently store raw DICOM pixel data once the Neural Engine has completed its inference. Only derived metadata and volumetric clinical findings are retained for the patient record lifecycle.

Terms of Service

Clinician Responsibility

The NeuroScanAI platform is a Clinical Decision Support (CDS) tool. It is not intended for autonomous diagnosis. Final diagnostic confirmation remains the sole responsibility of the licensed radiologist or attending physician.

FDA Disclosures

Class II Software-as-a-Medical-Device (SaMD)

NeuroScanAI has received 510(k) clearance for the analysis of cerebral glioma masses in adult patients. Use in pediatric populations or for spinal cord analysis is currently under investigational review.
